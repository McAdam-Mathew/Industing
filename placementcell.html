Placement Cell

The Placement and Training Cell, headed by a Senior Professor, helps students plan their career. It organizes Interviews and group discussions - to familiarize students with standard selection procedures Campus interviews - in association with large industrial and commercial firms and corporate bodies As a result, students of APCM have benefited every year with progressively remunerative and rewarding placements in reputed organizations.
